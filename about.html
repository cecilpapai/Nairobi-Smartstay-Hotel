Smart Hospitality, Authentic Kenyan Warmth

For twelve years, Nairobi SmartStay Hotel has redefined urban hospitality by seamlessly blending smart technology with authentic Kenyan warmth. Established in 2012 at the heart of Kenya's digital revolution, we've grown from a innovative concept into Nairobi's preferred choice for tech-savvy travelers. Our hotel combines effortless mobile check-ins and app-controlled rooms with the genuine "Karibu" spirit that makes visitors feel instantly at home.
